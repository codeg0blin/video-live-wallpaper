Bug fix release. 1.1.1 reduced but did not fully fix a MediaPlayer error that could repeat rapidly after certain screen lock/unlock sequences on some devices. The player is now properly discarded and re-created when this error occurs, instead of being reused in a broken state, which stops the repeat-error loop. A capped automatic retry (up to 3 attempts) is used before giving up, so your selected video is never cleared over a one-off playback hiccup.
