Pick any video from your device and set it as a looping live wallpaper. Applies to both home screen and lock screen simultaneously — this is how Android live wallpapers work on all devices.

Features:
* Pick a video using the system file picker
* Preview it looping before setting it
* Adjustable playback speed
* Choose between "fill" (crop to fit) and "fit" (show the whole frame) scaling
* One-tap wallpaper setting

This app contains no ads, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. It does not request internet access. The only data it stores is a reference to the video you choose, kept locally on your device.

Note: the system wallpaper picker may still present a Home / Lock / Both option depending on your device's launcher. This is controlled by your device manufacturer, not by this app — the video will play on whichever surfaces are selected.
