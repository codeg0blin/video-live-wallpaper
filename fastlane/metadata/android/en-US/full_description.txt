Pick any video from your device and set it as a looping live wallpaper, with separate controls for your home screen and lock screen.

Features:
* Pick a video using the system file picker
* Preview it looping before setting it
* Adjustable playback speed
* Choose between "fill" (crop to fit) and "fit" (show the whole frame) scaling
* Set as home screen wallpaper
* Set as lock screen wallpaper

This app contains no ads, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. It does not request internet access. The only data it stores is a reference to the video you choose, kept locally on your device.

Note: whether your device offers separate "home" and "lock screen" options, or only a combined option, depends on your launcher/device manufacturer's live wallpaper implementation, not on this app.
